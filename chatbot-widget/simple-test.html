This page demonstrates the absolute minimum code needed to add the chatbot widget to any website.

What you should see:

  • A blue circular chat button in the bottom-right corner
  • Click it to open an 800px wide panel
  • The panel should slide in from the right
  • Click the × button or chat button again to close

Integration Code:

<script src="https://raphaelfeliz.github.io/launch-iframe/widget.js" async></script>

That's it! Just one line of code to add the entire widget functionality.

The widget is completely self-contained and won't interfere with your existing page styles or functionality.
